About Myself

With over 35 years of experience in surgery, I have dedicated my career to advancing patient care through both traditional and innovative techniques. I am proud to have been a pioneer in the region for laparoscopic surgery and hernia mesh repair, both of which have significantly improved patient outcomes and recovery times. Throughout my career, I have gained extensive expertise in a variety of procedures, including prostate surgeries, appendectomies, and the treatment of conditions such as piles, perianal fistulas, and wound infections. I have also developed a deep understanding of managing abdominal emergencies, where swift and effective intervention is crucial. My approach to medicine combines years of experience with a commitment to staying at the forefront of surgical advancements, always aiming to provide the highest level of care for my patients.
